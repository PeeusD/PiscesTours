Puducherry


Puducherry, formerly Pondicherry , is a Union Territory of India formed out of four enclaves of former French India and named after the largest, Pondicherry. The Tamil name is (Puducherry), which means "New Town".[2] Historically known as Pondicherry (Pa??icceri), the territory changed its official name to Puducherry (Putucceri) in 2006.[3] It is also known as "The French Riviera of the East" (La Côte d'Azur de l'Est)
